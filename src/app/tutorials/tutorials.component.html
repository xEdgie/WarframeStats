Guide to status chance

Status Effect

A Status Effect, or what is more commonly called a 'Proc', is an additional effect which may be triggered at random by a hit from a weapon or Warframe ability.

These Procs can apply an effect to an enemy such as toxin which inflicts toxin damage upon the enemy.

Status Chance

Status Chance is the probability that a hit will inflict a status effect.

Status Duration

The base duration of the Proc will depend on its type, and whether the target is an enemy or another Tenno.

Warframe abilities usually have a high chance to proc, or in some cases, 100% chance to inflict their status effect

Combos

Status effects can be combined to create a new status effect, such as viral which can be created by combining the cold status effect with the toxin status effect
